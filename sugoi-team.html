Meet Our Team

The passionate professionals behind Sugoi Music Studio

FOUNDERS

Justin Edeluana

Founder & CEO

Visionary founder and CEO of Sugoi Music Studio. With over 10 years of experience in the music industry, Justin has built a reputation for excellence in audio production and artist development. His passion for music and commitment to quality has made Sugoi Music Studio the premier destination for musicians seeking professional recording services. Under his leadership, the studio has become a creative haven where artists can bring their musical visions to life with state-of-the-art equipment and expert guidance.

Alex Rodriguez

Audio Engineer

Expert audio engineer with a keen ear for detail and a passion for bringing out the best in every recording. Alex specializes in mixing and mastering, ensuring that every track meets the highest professional standards. With experience working with various genres from rock to electronic music, Alex brings versatility and technical expertise to every session.

Maria Santos

Producer

Creative producer and songwriter who helps artists shape their sound and realize their artistic vision. Maria has worked with both emerging and established artists, bringing fresh perspectives and innovative production techniques to every project. Her collaborative approach and musical intuition make her an invaluable part of the creative process.

David Kim

Studio Manager

Dedicated studio manager who ensures smooth operations and exceptional client experiences. David coordinates sessions, manages equipment maintenance, and provides technical support to artists and engineers. His attention to detail and commitment to service excellence keeps Sugoi Music Studio running at peak performance day in and day out.

Sarah Johnson

Vocal Coach & Session Singer

Professional vocal coach and accomplished session singer with over 8 years of experience. Sarah helps artists develop their vocal technique, stage presence, and performance skills. Her versatile voice has been featured on numerous recordings, and her teaching methods have helped countless artists unlock their full vocal potential.

Marcus Thompson

Mix Engineer & Sound Designer

Creative mix engineer and sound designer who specializes in crafting unique sonic landscapes. Marcus brings a fresh perspective to every mix, combining technical precision with artistic flair. His expertise spans multiple genres, from hip-hop and R&B to electronic and alternative rock.

Emily Chen

Mastering Engineer

Expert mastering engineer with a reputation for bringing out the best in every recording. Emily's meticulous approach and trained ear ensure that every track sounds polished and radio-ready. She works closely with artists and mixing engineers to achieve the perfect final sound that translates beautifully across all playback systems.
